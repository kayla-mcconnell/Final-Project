  • Why did you start Valkyrie Nails?
    To be able to provide quick, inexpensive, beautiful manicures to everyone.

  • Are these real nail polish?
    Yes, these nail wraps are 100% nail polish. They are produced by screen printing each layer to create designs.

  • Have your nail wraps been tested on animals?
    No, we love all animals and would never harm them in the making of our product!

  • Where are you based?
    Right here in the USA. Our headquarters are located in Salt Lake City, Utah.

  • Do you ship outside of the USA?
    Unfortunately, we do not ship internationally at this time.

  • Can I buy in bulk?
    Yes! Reach out to us on our "Contact Us" page and let us know how many you are looking to purchase and we can prepare a discounted rate.

    Shipping is always free!

    We accept returns for any damaged product within 30 days of purchase.
